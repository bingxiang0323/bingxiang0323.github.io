Origin Story

In a place far, far away, on a strange and mysterious planet, there existed an endless abyss of darkness. People struggled to survive in the darkness, their hearts filled with fear and uncertainty of the unknown.

.

In this dark world, an elderly sage was determined to find a power to guide people out of the darkness and restore light and hope. After a long period of exploration, he eventually discovered a mysterious and powerful entity—the Refrigerator God. This enigmatic being possessed powers beyond mortal understanding, capable of dispersing darkness and bringing warmth and peace.

.

The Refrigerator God was born out of the fear of the unknown, but it was not an evil entity. On the contrary, it was a warm and loving guardian. Determined to lead people to overcome the darkness and regain light and hope, the Refrigerator God infused its power into every refrigerator, turning them into sanctuaries for the soul, providing warmth and comfort to people.

.

From that moment on, the Refrigerator God became the guardian deity of the planet.
